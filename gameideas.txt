Game Ideas - Version Control Assignment (DIG 3779)

1. Prop Plane game; Seated game where you act as a pilot delivering packages to different areas of the map for money. But, to make ends meet, you have to turn to smuggling to pay the bills. The fun part is balancing doing normal jobs and smuggling to not get caught. Home base where you live is a hangar you can edit and customize.

Features:
- Cartoon animal society 
- 360 window view
- Dialogue options over the phone/radio
- Assistant that lives in same hangar 
- Dog fights with the police and other smugglers 

2. 3rd person action adventure that is based off of SkyHearts world of floating islands. Follows a different story line just uses heavy inspiration from the art, technology, and world.

Features:
- Combat
- Dialog Trees
- Travel with ships (goes to outer ship view) and character perspectives
- NO fast travel: part of the game is flying the ship well and maneuverability
- Customizable ships with money

3. Mortal Engines game. Based on the books and movie. Plays like EVE Online but less complex. The large cities move slowly and can engage in war with one another. It will be a PvPvE. 
PvPvE features:
- Not sure if it will be continuous play or on a server wipe basis?
- E will be small randomly generating consumable trading cities with fluctuating generation 
- Tectonic plate movement to open new areas of the world?
- Hell Diver like dev involvement and effect
- massive cities that factions can take down that come from the "north"

Playing features:
- Faster smaller leech cities and flying ships for reconnaissance and to see the map
- Tracking and infiltration into different cities
- Collecting resources to keep your city alive and joining factions/building cities together
